Terms and Conditions

At Blue Pepper Hostel, we strive to provide you with the best experience. These terms and conditions outline the rules and policies regarding bookings, payments, and services provided.

1. Bookings

Bookings are confirmed with a deposit payment. We reserve the right to cancel any booking if payment is not received.

2. Payments

Full payment for accommodation must be made at check-in. We accept payments in cash, credit cards, and bank transfers.

Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours before the arrival date to receive a full refund. Cancellations outside this timeframe may incur charges.

We expect all guests to respect our property rules. Smoking is not allowed inside the premises, and disruptive behavior may result in termination of the stay without a refund.

6. Contact

If you have any questions or require clarification regarding these terms and conditions, please contact us at contact@bluepepperhostel.com.
